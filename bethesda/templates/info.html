Dear family, friends & well-wishers,

Greetings in the precious name of Jesus Christ, our Lord, and Savior. I pray that this letter reaches you in good health and spirit. As we think of God’s goodness, we cannot help but say, “It's the Lord’s doing and its marvelous in our eyes!” -Psalm 118:23

This year, we celebrate our 45th Annual Church Anniversary. We have set aside the month of June 1-24, 2018 to celebrate, rejoice and give God thanks for bringing us thus far. We are excited to have reached this milestone. We would be honored if you would join us to rejoice, reflect and be an integral part of this momentous celebration.

Our church is diligently making strides to make a positive impact and changes in the communities of East Flatbush, Brownsville, East New York and the other surrounding areas in Brooklyn, New York. We believe that a church is not a church until it reaches and touches the lives of the people it serves. Hence we ask for your prayers, presence, and support in our efforts to reach mankind and serve his people with life-changing truths.
